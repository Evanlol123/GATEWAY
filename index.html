About

Welcome to G.A.T.E.W.A.Y.! (Group Advocating for Technological Empowerment and Web Accessibility for Youth) We are a group dedicated to advocating for technological empowerment and web accessibility for youth. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and provide opportunities for young people to thrive in the digital world.
