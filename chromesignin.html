Chrome signin: Get Unblocked Google

Steps:

  1. Go to "chrome://chrome-signin/"
  2. Click "ok" (bottom right)
  3. In the "Email or Phone" input box, type "google@d11.org"
  4. Click "Next" (bottom right)
  5. Verify the captcha if it's there
  6. Click "Sign-In Options" (below the "Sign In" box)
  7. Click "Sign in with GitHub"
  8. Click "Forgot password?"
  9. Click the cat icon at the top
  10. All the way at the top (It is cut in half and little to the right), click "Search GitHub"
  11. Type google then press enter
  12. Click "www.google.com"
  13. All Done Now enjoy!
